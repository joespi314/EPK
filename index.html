Artist Bio

Joseph Schwartz is a Chicago-based artist and songwriter pioneering a new path in music creation. He leverages AI as a collaborative tool, acting as a lyricist, producer, and musical interpreter. He pairs powerful lyrics—both his own and those of collaborators like Herman Bean—with AI-generated instrumentation and vocals to craft music that is both forward-thinking and deeply personal.

Unified by a focus on lyrics that provoke thought, his explorations fearlessly span genres—from rock and folk to pop and dance.
