Artist Bio

Joseph Schwartz is a Chicago-based artist and songwriter pioneering a new path in music creation. Anchored by the heavy melodic rock sound of his breakout release "Ready for Doom," Joseph leverages AI as a collaborative tool—acting as lyricist, producer, and musical interpreter.

He pairs powerful lyrics—both his own and those of collaborators like Herman Bean—with AI-generated instrumentation to craft music that is forward-thinking and deeply personal. While his current audience has rallied around his rock anthems, Joseph's vision fearlessly spans genres. From the political edge of rock to the storytelling of country and folk, his work is unified not by a single sound, but by a core focus on lyrics that provoke thought, spark conversation, and explore the human condition.
